About Me

I am a father, son and husband. Not necessarily always in that order, and with varying degrees of success. I am financial professional who secretly wants to work with computers instead of retirement models and legal plan documents. The hardest thing I ever learned is patience. The best gift from my parents was humility.

A few factoids about me:

  • I used to hate having red hair. I was always the kid who got caught because I was easily recognized. Later I liked having red hair because people would remember me more easily and I wouldn't have to embarrasingly re-introduce myself. Although it's a lot of work, I think I like being bald best, because it doesn't require me to have an opinion about my hair.
  • I graduated high school in a class of nine and a half people. The half is an exchange student who - after graduating here - had to return to Norway for another year of pre-collegiate education.
  • I lived in Colombia with one of my wife's aunts for 3 months to learn Spanish. My wife thinks this is so our kids would be raised bilingually and be able to communicate with her extended family in Colombia. Really it's so I could understand what her family was saying about her gringo husband.
  • My son is a black-belt candidate in Tae Kwon Do. He got me into the practice some years back. I think it's so he can boss me around, since he is my senior in the dojang.
  • My favorite type of music is in the extreme metal genres. A current favorite is Horrendous, a death metal band from Phildelphia.
